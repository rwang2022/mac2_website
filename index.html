Memory, Aging, and Cognitive Control (MAC2)

Help us explore the mysteries of the mind

Participate in Our Studies

We are looking for participants aged 18 and up from diverse backgrounds to join our memory and cognition studies. Our studies are safe, and we offer compensation for your time. Discover how you can contribute to advancing our understanding of the brain!

How to Sign Up: Scroll to bottom

General Information

Eligibility: Adults 18 and older, right-handed, with normal or corrected vision, and none of the following:

  • history of neurological disease or brain damage
  • history of drug or substance abuse
  • uncontrollable shaking

Compensation: $20 per hour for community members, 1 research credit per hour for students.

Study Information

Study 1: Understanding Memory Across Ages

Purpose: To explore how people understand their own memory, which helps us enhance educational outcomes and understanding the effects of aging.

Additional Eligibility: no MRI contraindications (metal objects in the body, claustrophobia, pregnancy)

Total Time

2.5 hours

Locations

  1. Binghamton University
  2. UHS Vestal MRI Center

First Visit

Brief cognitive tests and a survey (45 minutes)

Second Visit

MRI scan with memory and mathematical tests (90 minutes)

Study 2: Gut and Brain Health

Purpose: To understand how the gut microbiome influences brain health and cognitive performance, informing public health policies.

Additional Eligibility: no unremovable hair extensions or wigs

Total Time

3.5-4 hours

Locations

  1. Binghamton University
  2. UHS Vestal MRI Center (for some)

First Visit

Complete a survey, cognitive tasks, and a memory task.

Second Visit

Measurements taken with an MRI scan or a light-measuring cap.

At home task: Collect a stool sample and return it.
